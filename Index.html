Obsession

EXO's 6th Album 'OBSESSION' (EXO & X-EXO Ver.) will be released on November 27th.

The album features total 10 songs in various colors & genre, including powerful and catchy title song,

'Obsession', which will be out in both Korean and Chinese version. One of two covers (EXO & X-EXO Ver.)

will be supplied at random. The album includes 6 photo books, 1 folded poster (random 1 out of 6), 1

initial-only poster (folded, random 1 out of 7), 1 photo slide (random 1 out of 6), and 1 photo card

(random 1 out of 12). All are different per version. This is an official release from SM Entertainment

and will count towards Billboard and Gaon charts.
